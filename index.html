Core Values & Principles of Medical Mission

Love and Faith

We are guided by Christian principles and seek to love God and our neighbours, showing compassion and empathy to all.

Integrity and Professionalism

We uphold the highest standards of professionalism, accountability, and ethics in our work.

Mutual Respect

We recognise and respect the inherent dignity and potential of all individuals, working to improve their quality of life.

Solidarity

We stand in solidarity with affected communities, especially the more vulnerable amongst them, including women, children, and the elderly, providing them with hope and support for a better future.

Equality

We welcome and respect people of all races, ethnicities, religions, ages, genders, languages, and abilities, promoting inclusivity and equality.

Commitment to Excellence

We remain committed to our policies, objectives, and vision, adapting to changes and challenges with an open-minded and flexible approach; striving for continuous improvement, professional development, and the highest quality of healthcare and outcomes.

Mobile Clinics

Our team implemented two mobile clinics in April and August. We treated more than 300 patients. We provided essential healthcare services to remote and underserved areas. These mobile clinics enabled our team to offer treatments, vaccinations, and general health check-ups to hundreds of individuals who otherwise would have had limited access to medical care.

Maternal Services

We trained women in Kapoeta North County on menstrual and general hygiene (Health Promotion). We distributed soaps. These services also included educational sessions on maternal health, nutrition, and infant care. Our team empowered women with the knowledge to ensure healthier pregnancies and deliveries.

Our Vision

To bring hope and healing to the most vulnerable communities in South Sudan and East Africa, transforming lives holistically by providing healthcare services and humanitarian aid, with a focus on promoting human dignity.









Our Mission

Medical Mission aims to transform the unreached communities in sub-Saharan East Africa through provision of subsidised medical services, mobile surgery and through training and education. We strive to impact these communities with the true message of hope and love, centred around the teachings of Jesus Christ. We envision partnering with local communities and their leaders, networking together with: government services; churches; medical teams from East Africa alongside those from developed countries on short term medical mission. Together we will work to address the critical healthcare needs of the most marginalised populations. Our actions are guided by our commitment to serve others and Jesus’ prayer in Matthew 6:9-10 (“Our Father in heaven, hallowed be your name. Your kingdom come, your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven”) .
